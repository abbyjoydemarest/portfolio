GenArt

Before we delved into the world of JavaScript one of our group assignments was to develop an app that gave people the opportunity to post links to websites related to a specific area of interest. My project partner and I both love data driven and generative art, so GenArt was born. This projct is important to me because it was our first group project. We did a lot of pair programming, which was a new concept to me, but I found it to be extremly valuable and impactful.

Calculator


The calculator project was one of my most favorite individual projects to do at Momentum Learning, Inc. This was our first stab at JavaScript and I liked being able to use JavaScript to enhance the user's experience. When the user clicks on a number on the calculator the background turns a shade of blue that correlates to that number (ex: 0 is the lightest blue, 9 is the darkest blue).
